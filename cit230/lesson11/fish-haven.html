Breaking News

Two young teens who were hit by a drunk driver are now starting a company that offers comfort and support to victims and their families. They will be opening the doors August 2020. All donations will go straight to the cost of medical care for those who are currently healing or who are in hospitals now. Their statement, "We hope we can start by spreading hope and comfort to those who have suffered these kinds of awful events and hope to make a difference. Maybe we can come together to create better laws to protect our loved ones from such catashtrophes."
